Network Wallet is an agentic multi-chain wallet that provides seamless cross-chain transactions without manual bridging.

FEATURES:

• SEAMLESS CROSS-CHAIN
Transfer assets between chains without switching networks or manual bridging. The wallet automatically handles cross-chain operations through EIL (Ethereum Interop Layer).

• INTENT-BASED TRANSACTIONS
Express what you want to accomplish and let the wallet figure out the optimal execution path through OIF (Open Intents Framework).

• ACCOUNT ABSTRACTION
ERC-4337 smart accounts with social recovery, gas sponsorship, and batched transactions.

• HARDWARE WALLET SUPPORT
Secure your assets with Ledger and Trezor hardware wallets via Bluetooth.

• AI-POWERED ASSISTANCE
Get help understanding transactions, optimizing gas, and managing your portfolio.

• MULTI-CHAIN SUPPORT
Supports all major EVM chains including Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, and more.

• DEFI INTEGRATION
Seamlessly interact with DEXs, lending protocols, and other DeFi applications.

• PRIVACY FIRST
Your keys, your crypto. All sensitive data is stored locally and encrypted with biometric protection.

SUPPORTED NETWORKS:
- Ethereum Mainnet
- Base
- Arbitrum One
- Optimism
- Polygon
- And many more...

SECURITY:
- Face ID / Touch ID authentication
- Hardware wallet support

- Open source and audited
