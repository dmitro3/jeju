Network Wallet is an agentic multi-chain wallet that provides seamless cross-chain transactions without manual bridging.

<b>Features:</b>

• <b>Seamless Cross-Chain:</b> Transfer assets between chains without switching networks or manual bridging. The wallet automatically handles cross-chain operations through EIL (Ethereum Interop Layer).

• <b>Intent-Based Transactions:</b> Express what you want to accomplish and let the wallet figure out the optimal execution path through OIF (Open Intents Framework).

• <b>Account Abstraction:</b> ERC-4337 smart accounts with social recovery, gas sponsorship, and batched transactions.

• <b>Hardware Wallet Support:</b> Secure your assets with Ledger and Trezor hardware wallets.

• <b>AI-Powered Assistance:</b> Get help understanding transactions, optimizing gas, and managing your portfolio.

• <b>Multi-Chain Support:</b> Supports all major EVM chains including Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, and more.

• <b>DeFi Integration:</b> Seamlessly interact with DEXs, lending protocols, and other DeFi applications.

• <b>Privacy First:</b> Your keys, your crypto. All sensitive data is stored locally and encrypted.

<b>Supported Networks:</b>
- Ethereum Mainnet
- Base
- Arbitrum One
- Optimism
- Polygon
- And many more...

<b>Security:</b>
- Biometric authentication
- Hardware wallet support
- Encrypted local storage
- Open source and audited
